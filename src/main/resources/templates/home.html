Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Usage

RWE Platform collects and processes your data to provide personalized efficiency recommendations. We only collect information necessary for the service and do not share your personal data with third parties.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (username, email)
  • Vehicle selection and preferences
  • Route planning data
  • Usage analytics (anonymized)

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide personalized route recommendations
  • Calculate fuel efficiency based on your vehicle
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important service updates

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We regularly review and update our security practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us if you have any questions about your data or privacy.
